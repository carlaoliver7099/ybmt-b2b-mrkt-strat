# 📐 DEPTH STANDARD v1.0 — The 10/10 Quality Gate

**Locked by:** Carla Oliver, Acting CMO (ex-Top 4: Accenture / IBM / CapGemini / Deloitte)
**Effective:** 2026-06-05
**Purpose:** Define exactly what 10/10 means for each of the 11 workstreams. No workstream is declared complete until every checklist item is ticked. No layer advances until the layer below passes the gate.

---

## THE STANDARD IN ONE SENTENCE

> **A junior analyst at YDT or YBMT picks up this work, reads it cold, and executes against it with zero further questions.**

If that test fails, the workstream is not 10/10. Full stop.

---

## THE 8 LAYERS OF DEPTH (Every workstream must demonstrate all 8)

| # | Layer | Test |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Framework** | Is the methodology cited (3+ named sources) and explained? |
| 2 | **Application to YMT** | Is it instantiated specifically for YMT, not generic? |
| 3 | **Operating Detail** | Are the exact tactics, scripts, templates, rules, thresholds present? |
| 4 | **Edge Cases** | Are the "what if it doesn't fit" exceptions documented? |
| 5 | **Measurement** | Is the success metric defined with a target? |
| 6 | **Templates** | Are the deployable artefacts present (copy, schemas, dashboards)? |
| 7 | **Failure Modes** | Are the risks + hedges named? |
| 8 | **References** | Are the specific sources cited inline at point of use? |

## THE QUALITY GATE PROCESS

For each workstream, before declaring 10/10:

1. **Build** the workstream with the checklist visible
2. **Tick** each box only when actually delivered (not just acknowledged)
3. **Self-audit** — walk the checklist top to bottom, look for any "I'll come back to this" or "outline-only" items
4. **The Junior Analyst Test** — could a junior at YDT execute against this with zero questions?
5. **Only then** — declare 10/10 and proceed

If any box is empty or any test fails: **the workstream is not 10/10 and does not pass the gate.**
